Critical Diagnostic: Feature Target Data Leakage Detected in Notebook

The Linear Regression model in the original notebook reports a perfect **0.00% Mean Absolute Percentage Error (MAPE)**. This occurs due to data leakage: the Rolling_Mean feature was computed including the current year's sales. Since the model utilizes Sales_Lag1, Sales_Lag2, and Rolling_Mean, it learns the algebraic relationship: Sales_Cr = 3 * Rolling_Mean - Sales_Lag1 - Sales_Lag2. 📊 Havells India Ltd Sales Forecasting Project Summary

1. Project Background

This analytics interface represents an internship project evaluating real industry financial statements for Havells India Ltd (NSE: HAVELLS). The objective was to predict sales for the next three years using a combination of traditional linear models and machine learning models.

Data was obtained from public databases via Screener.in, containing two core subsets:

  • Annual Sales: FY2017 to FY2026 (10 years)
  • Quarterly Sales: Dec 2023 to Mar 2026 (10 quarters)

2. Explored ML Models

  • Linear Regression: Explores simple temporal trends. Extrapolates historical growth forward linearly.
  • Facebook Prophet: Tailored time-series forecasting model developed by Meta. Captures seasonality trends and provides lower/upper confidence bounds.
  • Random Forest & XGBoost Regressors: Ensemble decision tree models capturing non-linear interactions and lag relationships.

3. Core Findings & Insights

  1. Growth Durability: Havells has maintained a high-growth rate with an actual CAGR of 15.6% over the past decade. The only YoY sales dip occurred during FY2020 (-6.3%) due to pandemic disruptions.
  2. Target Data Leakage Analysis: The rolling average calculation in the original training script incorporated current-year sales, creating a perfect prediction model in validation but failing in external extrapolation. To resolve this, forecasts were retrained purely on absolute temporal sequence variables.
  3. Prophet vs. Linear Regression: Prophet predicts sales growing to ₹21,357 Cr by FY2029, whereas Linear Regression projects ₹27,964 Cr. Prophet is likely more reliable as it avoids scaling outliers and dampens extreme trends.
